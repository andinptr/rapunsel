SCHOOL FULL ADDRESS

Jl. Rajawali V Jl. Anggrek Raya No.29, RT.005/RW.023, Kayuringin Jaya, Kec. Bekasi Sel., Kota Bks, Jawa Barat 17144.
Teratai Putih Global 2 Bekasi Vocational School is one of the Vocational High Schools established by the Bekasi Global Teratai Foundation. Like SMKs in general in Indonesia, the school education period at Teratai Putih Global 2 Bekasi Vocational School is taken within three academic years, starting from Class X to Class XII. The curriculum applied here is the 2013 curriculum with a time allocation of 10 - 12 hours of lessons which take place from Monday to Friday. Before starting class hours, students usually hold Al-Quran readings led by the teacher on duty.
